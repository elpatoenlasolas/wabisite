#1 Concept

At SitStay, dogs graduate after having learned and experienced a series of fun exercises that' wi'll follow their development in the different stages of their growth.
For this reason, we decided to create a repertoir of fun and attractive designs that complement the identity of this brand.


Some of the work done on this project:
  • Brand Concept
  • Graphic System
  • Web Design
  • Audiovisual Production
  • Social Media Graphics

#2 Development

The concept of "Graduation" is represented by throwing the hat up in the air.
The versatility of our designs allows the products to be a perfect fit for modern marketing.


We produced a high quality video production for the introduction of the website, with original music and worked side by side with the trainer to capture the essence of the puppies.
